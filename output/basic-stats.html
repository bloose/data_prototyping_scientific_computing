Script to calculate Basic Statistics¶

Equation for the mean: $\mu_x = \sum_{i=1}^{N}\frac{x_i}{N}$¶

Equation for the standard deviation: $\sigma_x = \sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{N}\left(x_i - \mu \right)^2}\frac{1}{N-1}$¶
